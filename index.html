Hello! My name is Vinay, and I am currently a graduate student specializing in Medical Imaging and Informatics at the School of Medical Science and Technology, Indian Institue of Technology Kharagpur. Prior to Master's, I received my bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communications Engineering from Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies, RK Valley in 2021. I am a co-founder and CTO of a agriculture robotics startup named TechInAg that has received a seed grant of 330K INR from MANAGE Hyderabad. My research interests include Computer Vision and Deep Learning for Medical Imaging and Ecological Conservation.
